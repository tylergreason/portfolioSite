Hi, I'm Tyler Greason

My background is in commercial building energy efficiency, which taught me to approach problems holistically and to always strive for efficiency. When programming just as a hobby stopped sufficing my desire to create, I took the plunge and joined Flatiron to become a full stack web developer.
I enjoy writing clean, efficient, and functional code. I am happiest working on the front end, creating a user experience that is as much a pleasure to look at as it is to use. Check out my latest project here: www.stufftodomap.com
Key competencies: Javascript, CSS, HTML, React, Redux, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, git.
